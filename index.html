BIO

Hai Chien Pham is currently the Head of the Department of Construction Engineering and Management (DCEM) of the Faculty of Civil Engineering at Ton Duc Thang University, Viet Nam.
He graduated from Chung-Ang University, Seoul, South Korea in terms of the construction management area in 2018. During all those years, he was proactively in the social activities of students and/or others in Viet Nam. He used to be the President of the Vietnamese Students Association in Korea (VSAK), Senior Vice President of the Association of Vietnamese Community in Korea (AVCK), and member of the Executive Board of the Viet Nam National Union of Students (VNUS).
Before pursuing his Ph.D. program in Korea, Dr. Chien had more than 15 years of solid experience in construction management. Dr. Chien graduated from Hochiminh City University of Technology in 1999, lately mastered construction management at the Asian Institute of Technology in Thailand in 2012, and had obtained PMP Certification (issued by Project Management Institute PMI).
His research interests consist of Building Information Modeling (BIM), Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Photoreality, and mobile computing, which focus on technology-enhanced applications for construction education, training, and management.
